Masterclasses

Whisky Masterclass

Nick & Nora’s thoroughly enjoys hosting classes; whether you are looking to hone your knowledge or just have some fun. These special occasions are great to do with a group of friends or a fun way to encourage team building amongst your staff. Masterclasses are available for groups of 6-25 guests and include 5 x 15ml whiskies for $75 per person. Each session is hosted by one of our talented bar tenders and run for approximately 90 minutes.

Cocktail Massterclass

Cocktail Masterclasses can be held as a private class or as part of an afternoon or evening event. All sessions are hosted by one of our passionate bar tenders and run for approximately 90 minutes. Masterclasses are available for 6-20 guests and include 3 cocktails and light snacks for $85 per person.
